Our Journey Through Time

The Beginning (1994)

Founded by visionary leader Mr. Rajesh Sharma, Shiloh Sneha began as a small security service provider in Vijayawada with just 5 security guards and a dream to make a difference.

Starting with local businesses and residential complexes, we quickly gained reputation for reliability and professionalism in the security industry.

Growth & Recognition (2000-2010)

Expanded operations across 15 districts in Andhra Pradesh. Established first formal training center and began government partnerships for youth employment programs.

Received state government recognition for outstanding contribution to youth employment and security services excellence.

Innovation Era (2010-2020)

Introduced technology-driven security solutions, advanced training methodologies, and established partnerships with major corporations across South India.

Achieved milestone of training and placing over 3,000 youth while serving 200+ corporate clients simultaneously.

Digital Transformation (2020-Present)

Embraced digital technologies, online training modules, and AI-driven security solutions. Launched ambitious goal of training 1.8 lakh youth.

Today, we stand as Andhra Pradesh’s leading security training and services provider with 30 years of proven excellence.
